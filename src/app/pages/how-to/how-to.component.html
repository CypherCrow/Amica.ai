Questions

The game features two types of questions: email and chat.

Email questions will take the form of an email from a colleague or an attacker in disguise, both detailing a message concerning a cyberattack.
Chat questions will involve a character asking you a question regarding a cyberdefense practice.

Answering these questions will involve a number of choices that will be present. What choice you will make will have consequences, whether for better or worse.

Starting the Game

To start the game, finish the carousel in the homepage.
