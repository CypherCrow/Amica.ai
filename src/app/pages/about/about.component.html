About Amica.AI

Cybersecurity is a discipline that needs to be taken seriously. And new defenses comes new threats.

Amica.AI immerses you in a fictional story-driven event in a fictional enterprise AI company where you are to protect the company's assests and individuals. Involving multiple applications connected to one site, this game sets of a virtually realistic feel of working corporate environment.

Through this environment, the game promotes cybersecurity via an immersive fashion. Interacting with fictional characters, both good and bad, in a realistic setting gets your mind piqued with building and enhacing your awareness of cyberattacks and defenses.

Go on ahead and check it out for yourself. Or consider playing it later when you're not too busy.
